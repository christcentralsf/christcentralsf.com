Who can I contact if I'm new to CCPC?

You can contact us. Tell us about yourself, and on your first day at CCPC, look for the greeter or table with the Welcome sign directly after service. You'll meet other newcomers along with our Newcomer Committee. A free lunch will be waiting for you!

What is the vision of CCPC?

Our vision is on our vision page, and you can learn more about our reformed church from our denomination, the Korean American Presbyterian Church (KAPC), which adopted the twelve points of our faith, the Westminster Confession of Faith, the Westminster Shorter Catechism, the Westminster Larger Catechism, and the Book of Church Order.

What time is worship service at CCPC?

Our service at CCPC begins promptly at 9:50am every Sunday unless specified otherwise. We also have a college ministry, Fellowship in Christ, Berkeley (FICB), that meet most Fridays on campus. Our Youth (high school students) meet most Friday nights downstairs at church.

How do I get to CCPC?

CCPC is located at 50 Northridge Drive, Daly City, CA 94015. Visit our directions page for instructions, and please be considerate of our neighbors! Better yet, carpool. Contact us if you need a ride.

What programs are offered for children at CCPC?

We have several programs depending on your children's demographics. Most children attend our Sunday School classes during our regular service.

How do I join a community group at CCPC?

We provide community groups for college students, working adults, and married couples. They meet regionally all over the Bay Area and usually Tuesday through Thursday nights after 6:30pm. Our college students participate in community groups local to their campuses in Berkeley. contact us if you'd like to join a community group.

How do I learn about upcoming events at CCPC?

There are many different events each month, but only four primary ways of staying in tune with these events at CCPC: [1] by reading the program available during Sunday service; [2] by reading our online news; [3] by joining a community group; and [4] by joining our mailing lists.

How can I serve God and His people at CCPC?

We're always in need of servants and we really appreciate your active interest. Check our online news for opportunities or contact us if you know how you'd like to serve.

How do I remove myself from a CCPC mailing list?

We have two mailing lists: our announce mailing list is only used for official announcements and events while our community mailing list is used freely by all members of our congregation usually for housing, job searches, concerts, unofficial outings, etc. We discourage spam, solicitations, and meaningless chatter on both mailing lists. If you'd like to unsubscribe from either mailing address, we'd like to apologize for any inconvenience we've caused you. You can contact us or unsubscribe by sending an e-mail from your subscribed address to community+unsubscribe@christcentralsf.com and/or announce+unsubscribe@christcentralsf.com.

Who can I contact about weddings at CCPC?

Please contact us so we can provide you with more details directly.

What name should we use? Christ Central or CCPC?

Though we use CCPC for convenience and brevity, we would prefer to be known as Christ Central as that lets people better know who we are.
