Speaker

Dr. Julie Hayden, Psy.D.

Dr. Julie Hayden is a licensed psychologist, the Executive Director of Rhombus, the co-chair of the San Diego Marriage and Family Therapists Consortium and and current faculty and former Dean of the Graduate School of Behavioral Sciences at Southern California Seminary.

Her clinical experience includes working with chronic mental illness, substance and sexual addictions, adolescents, families, PTSD, depression, anxiety, domestic violence, and more. Her goal is to increase education and skills to help people live healthy lives and strengthen families. Dr. Hayden has over 14 years experience counseling, teaching, and training, and she believes truth comes from the inerrant Word of God and any effective tool will be in line with a biblical worldview.

Sessions

Session 1 "Clash of Worldviews"

This session will unfold a powerful perspective on a Biblical worldview and how a clear understanding of this will protect you, your family, your church and community from spiritual warfare. Dr. Hayden will unfold the science and psychology of mental illness, addictions, divorce and other difficulties families face in context of biblical truth. Attendees will feel armed with tools for better protecting their families and an awareness of where this battle may arise in the home.

Session 2 "Communication Myth Busters"

We are all taught communication is a key to a healthy relationship and yet, do we really know what this looks like. You may be surprised by what you don't know about healthy communication is a relationship. In this session, Dr. Hayden will share tools for fighting fair, conflict-resolution, and increasing intimacy through exposing commonly believed myths about communication.

Session 3 "Parenting with 20/20 Vision"

We all have a list of what we would go back and redo if possible, but rarely do we have the chance to know ahead of time what to adjust. In this session, Dr. Julie Hayden will share the pitfalls, mistakes, and patterns to avoid in parenting and the healthy alternatives to give your children the best opportunity.
