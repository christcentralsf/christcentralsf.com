Ministries

Family Life Ministry

Building Up the Church Through Strong Families.

Christ Central Family Life Ministry (FLM) exists to encourage, equip and strengthen marriages and families to bring honor and glory to our Father in heaven. We desire to strengthen, to challenge, to support, to mobilize and to evangelize families in the Bay Area. We believe in the life-transforming power of the Gospel as the only hope for renewing and restoring families.

Forum

Connecting the Christian Faith with Everyday Life.

CCPC Forum is a ministry that creates a platform to discuss, apply and connect the Gospel of Jesus Christ in our everyday lives. This ministry is open to post-college, pre-marriage individuals who all desire to glorify God in diverse contexts, vocations and callings in life.

Fellowship in Christ, Berkeley

Fellowship in Christ Berkeley (FiCB) is the campus ministry of Christ Central Presbyterian Church. As a local church, we believe that campus ministry plays an important role in the life and mission of Christ in the Bay Area. In FiCB, we are committed to teach, baptize and equip disciples of Jesus Christ through a Gospel centered fellowship within the 4+ years of college life.

Youth Group

Christ Central's youth ministry is unique in that it is a joint collaborative effort with our mother church (KCPC). Our goal is that CCPC's youth (grades 7-12) will come to grow in the grace of Christ and be full in the knowledge of him, and share in the holy communal life of the church in Christ.

Children's Ministry

The Children's Ministry provides worship and biblical instruction that is faithful and age-appropriate (K-6th grade). We exist to love, teach and inspire in order that they and their families will love God, lead like Christ, and confidently live the Christian faith.

Nursery

Christ Central Nursery provides care for the youngest members of our family (babies through pre-K). We strive to reflect the love of God in a nurturing and safe environment centered around the gospel.

Pastors & Staff

Pastor Benjamin Kim

Pastor Ben is happily married to Hetty, and they have two wonderful children, Kylee and Marcus, who remind them of God's amazing grace. He enjoys good books, most sports, good food, and spending time with his family. His simple goal in life is to preach Christ and Him crucified. He received his M.Div. from Westminster Seminary California and was ordained by the KAPC in 2000.

Pastor In Kim

In Kim was raised in Santa Barbara, California. He graduated with a BA in English from Westmont College in 2004 and worked for Special Needs education through 2007. In pursued his internal call to pastoral ministry at Westminster Seminary California, where he received his M.Div. in 2011. As an ordained pastor at CCPC, In Kim enjoys serving directly with campus ministry, "Forum" and community groups. His other interests include attending live concerts, playing soccer and appreciating God's common grace.

Martin Kang

Youth Group Director. Martin grew up in the San Gabriel Valley in Southern California and also in Seoul, Korea. He attended UC Berkeley and continues to reside in the city of Berkeley. Growing up as a fraternal twin son of a pastor/seminary professor, he came to personally know Christ as a sophomore in high school at a Christian club prayer meeting. He has worked in graphic production and also at a tech start-up in San Francisco. He enjoys writing in his Bible, reading Christian books and the NYTimes, and listening to sermons and NPR. He is particularly interested in art forms that communicate the gospel to the culture around him and in finding ways to continually revive and reform the church. He plans to attend seminary in the near future.
