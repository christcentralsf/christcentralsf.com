Counseling & Care

God is in the business of caring for his people. Some people need more care than others. As a church we provide pastoral Christian counseling, references to professional Christian counselors in the Bay Area, and Gospel resources for your own personal growth.

Care request form

What we believe

You are not alone.

“Praise be to the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of compassion and the God of all comfort, who comforts us in all our troubles, so that we can comfort those in any trouble with the comfort we ourselves receive from God.”

2 Corinthians 1:3-4

You are loved.

“We love because he first loved us.”

1 John 4:19

There is hope.

“No, in all these things we are more than conquerors through him who loved us. For I am convinced that neither death nor life, neither angels nor demons, neither the present nor the future, nor any powers, neither height nor depth, nor anything else in all creation, will be able to separate us from the love of God that is in Christ Jesus our Lord.”

Romans 8:37-39

Care is a web, not a passing of a baton.

We support the hurting and suffering with a team of:

  • pastoral — for biblical & spiritual counseling
  • peer — for a friend & prayer
  • professional — for licensed, Christian therapists in the Bay Area
